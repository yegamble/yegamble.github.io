Generic Certification

No or very little knowledge of identity. This certification does not pass the minimum set of specifications for key signing.


Persona Certification

Weak knowledge of identity and limited personal details such as residency and limited knowledge of associates. This certification does not pass the minimum set of specifications for key signing.
